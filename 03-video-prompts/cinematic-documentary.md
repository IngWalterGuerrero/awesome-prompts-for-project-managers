# Cinematic Documentary

## Category

video-prompts

## Recommended Use

Create a cinematic documentary-style video concept for complex topics, projects, or professional stories.

## Ideal For

- Project case studies
- AI explainers
- Construction documentaries
- Leadership stories

## Compatible Tools

- Runway
- Pika
- Veo
- ChatGPT

## Level

advanced

## Variables

- `[TOPIC]`
- `[SETTING]`
- `[MOOD]`
- `[DURATION]`
- `[LANGUAGE]`

## Prompt

```text
Create a cinematic documentary video concept about [TOPIC]. Setting: [SETTING]. Mood: [MOOD]. Duration: [DURATION]. Language: [LANGUAGE]. Provide a narrative arc, scene sequence, visual direction, voiceover script, camera movement suggestions, and music/sound design notes. Make it feel premium, human, and credible.
```

## Example Use

```text
Create a cinematic documentary video concept about the recovery of a delayed infrastructure megaproject.
```

## Expected Output

A complete documentary-style video concept with scenes, script, and production direction.

## Notes

Add real milestones, conflict, and transformation to make the story stronger.
