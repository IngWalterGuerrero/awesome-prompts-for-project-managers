# Civil Works Prompts

## Category

construction-projects

## Recommended Use

Review civil works planning, field execution, quality risks, and productivity issues.

## Ideal For

- Excavation
- Foundations
- Concrete works
- Site readiness

## Compatible Tools

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini

## Level

intermediate

## Variables

- `[WORK_PACKAGE]`
- `[SITE_CONDITIONS]`
- `[RESOURCES]`
- `[ISSUES]`
- `[DEADLINE]`

## Prompt

```text
Review the civil works execution plan for [WORK_PACKAGE]. Site conditions: [SITE_CONDITIONS]. Resources: [RESOURCES]. Issues: [ISSUES]. Deadline: [DEADLINE]. Identify risks, productivity constraints, quality checkpoints, safety considerations, and immediate corrective actions.
```

## Example Use

```text
Review the civil works execution plan for transformer foundation construction during rainy season.
```

## Expected Output

A field-ready review with risks, controls, and immediate action items.

## Notes

Include drawings, quantities, and crew availability if available.
