# Risk Control Prompts

## Category

construction-projects

## Recommended Use

Identify, assess, and control project risks in construction and infrastructure environments.

## Ideal For

- Risk workshops
- Project reviews
- Executive reporting
- Recovery planning

## Compatible Tools

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini

## Level

advanced

## Variables

- `[PROJECT_CONTEXT]`
- `[RISK_LIST]`
- `[IMPACTS]`
- `[CONTROLS]`
- `[REPORTING_FORMAT]`

## Prompt

```text
Review the risk control status for this construction project. Project context: [PROJECT_CONTEXT]. Risk list: [RISK_LIST]. Impacts: [IMPACTS]. Current controls: [CONTROLS]. Reporting format: [REPORTING_FORMAT]. Prioritize risks, assess control effectiveness, identify gaps, propose mitigation actions, and prepare an executive risk summary.
```

## Example Use

```text
Review the risk control status for a substation project with procurement delays, permit constraints, and commissioning risk.
```

## Expected Output

A risk control review with prioritization, gaps, mitigations, and executive summary.

## Notes

Use probability, impact, owner, due date, and trigger fields when possible.
